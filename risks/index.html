Overview

Ant investor Ltd is a private investment company, and as such, there are risks you need to be aware of. It’s important that you understand and are comfortable with these risks before you decide to contribute any amount with us. If you’re in any doubt, we always recommend getting in touch with a qualified financial adviser. You should also read the terms and conditions before you invest too.

The risk

Ant investor puts your contribution at risk whenever we go out to invest. By working with us you share this risk proportionately as you share in the benefits. We cannot guarantee the market place at any time hence an acceptance of possible loss allows you to only contribute what you can afford to loose.

Your contributions to the working capital of Ant Investor is put to work by buying and developing businesses and real estate from whose revenue we share proportionately. In the event you decide to withdraw your contributions, we or other clients will buy back this as soon as we can and give back your share. We cannot guarantee instant access though. You will also incur a cost if your contributions have not crossed their maturity period.

When you make your contribution it could take some time to invest and during this wait period the contribution may not generate an income.
