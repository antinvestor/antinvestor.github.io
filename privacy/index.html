Privacy policy

Ant Investor Ltd collects and uses personal information generated from our website and our online products and services. This document explains how we use this information.

1. Background

1.1 Ant Investor Limited respects the privacy of our clients and we are committed to keeping all your Personal Data secure. This Privacy Policy governs the handling of Personal Data by Ant Investor in the course of carrying on commercial activities.

1.2 We use the following definitions in this Privacy Policy:

  • “Ant Investor”, “we or “us” means Ant Investor Limited.
  • “Personal Data” means any data which relates to a living individual who can be identified from that data.

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Your Personal Data may be stored and processed by us in the following ways and for the following purposes:

  • allow you to use and access the functionality provided to support Ant Investor Products;
  • enable you submit your application for Ant Investor Products, where applicable;
  • set up users to use the the investor portal and/or the broker portal;
  • statistical monitoring and analysis of our systems and for the on-going adaptation and improvement of the solutions provided;
  • to send you select direct marketing communications to ensure that you are aware of new products and special offers that may be of interest to you;
  • for in-depth threat analysis;
  • for the management and administration of our business;
  • in order to comply with and in order to assess compliance with applicable laws, rules and regulations, and internal policies and procedures; or

4.2 We will take steps to ensure that the Personal Data is accessed only by employees of Ant Investor that have a need to do so for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

5. International Transfers Of Personal Data

5.1 Ant Investor is a global business. Our customers and our operations are spread around the world. As a result we collect and transfer Personal Data on a global basis. That means that we may transfer your Personal Datato locations outside of your country.

6. Your Rights

6.1 In all the above cases in which we collect, use or store your Personal Data, you may have the following rights and, in most cases, you can exercise them free of charge. These rights include:

  • the right to obtain information regarding the processing of your Personal Data and access to the Personal Data which we hold about you;
  • the right to withdraw your consent to the processing of your Personal Data at any time. Please note, however, that we may still be entitled to process your Personal Data if we have another legitimate reason for doing so. For example, we may need to retain Personal Data to comply with a legal obligation;
  • the right to request that we rectify your Personal Data if it is inaccurate or incomplete;
  • the right to request that we erase your Personal Data in certain circumstances. Please note that there may be circumstances where you ask us to erase your Personal Data but we are legally entitled to retain it;
  • the right to object to, or request that we restrict, our processing of your Personal Data in certain circumstances. Again, there may be circumstances where you object to, or ask us to restrict, our processing of your Personal Data but we are legally entitled to refuse that request; and
